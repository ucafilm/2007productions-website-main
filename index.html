About 2007

Where Stories Get Weird & Magic Happens

We are 2007 Productions, a collective of creative rebels who believe that normal is the enemy of memorable. Founded on the principle that the best stories live in the weird spaces between logic and magic.

Our journey began with a simple question: "What if we made it weirder?" This philosophy has guided us through countless projects, from mind-bending films to webisodes that make people question reality.

We don't just create content; we craft experiences that linger. Our team of visionaries, rule-breakers, and creative alchemists work together to turn impossible ideas into Tuesday afternoons.

From our state-of-the-art studio to remote locations around the globe, we bring stories to life with surgical precision and controlled explosion. Every pixel, every beat, every frame is crafted with obsessive attention to detail.

Whether we're directing narrative-driven pieces, capturing reality through a lens that makes it cooler than reality, or building sonic worlds where feelings live, we guarantee weirdness in everything we create.

Join us on this beautiful journey of controlled creative mayhem. Where chaos becomes symphony, deadlines become art, and your wildest ideas become reality.
